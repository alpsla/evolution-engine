## GDPR: Do We Need an EU Lawyer?

**No, not necessarily.** A US-based attorney with GDPR expertise is sufficient for our Privacy Policy. Key points:
- For a $19/month dev analytics CLI, GDPR exposure is relatively low
- We need the policy to accurately describe data collection and EU user rights
- Only need EU-specific counsel if establishing EU presence or appointing an Article 27 Representative
- Have a US tech/privacy lawyer flag anything requiring EU-specific advice
